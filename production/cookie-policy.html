ReachaGlobal Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on ReachaGlobal.com (the "Website").

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to enable a website to function or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How do we use cookies?

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and expire once you close your browser. They are used to remember your preferences within a single session, such as items in your shopping cart or login information.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time after you close your browser. They are used to help us understand how you use our website and to personalize your experience. For example, we may use a persistent cookie to remember your language preference.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use analytics cookies to collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and how long you spend on each page. This information helps us improve the performance of our website.
  • Third-party Cookies: We may also use third-party cookies, such as those from social media platforms, to provide you with certain features and functionalities on our website.

Your cookie choices

You have a number of options regarding cookies:

  • You can choose to disable all or some cookies by adjusting your browser settings. To learn more about how to manage cookies, visit the websites of your browser providers.
  • You can opt out of some third-party cookies by visiting the websites of the third-party cookie providers.
  • Please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.
